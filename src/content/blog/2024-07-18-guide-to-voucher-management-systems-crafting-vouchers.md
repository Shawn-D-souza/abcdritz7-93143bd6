Vouchers are a powerful marketing tool to attract new customers, reward loyalty, and boost sales. However, effectively managing these vouchers can be a challenge. This is where a robust voucher management system (VMS) comes in.

##   

## What is a voucher management system (VMS)?

Voucher management systems are software applications that [automate and streamline](/blog/no-code-automation-how-quickload-saves-120k) issuing, tracking, and redeeming of promotional codes or coupons. These systems help businesses with their voucher code campaigns. They provide tools to create, distribute, and track digital or physical vouchers. This ensures that businesses can effectively manage the lifecycle of their vouchers.

##   

## Benefits of using a voucher management system

*   **Easy coupon campaigns:** VMS helps create and send coupons to certain customer groups or for specific promotions.
    
*   **Custom and unique coupon generation:** Generate unique coupon codes with changeable details like validity periods, usage limits, and applicability.
    
*   **Multi-channel distribution:** Distribute digital coupons through email or SMS, or integrate them with your website or app.
    
*   **Track and measure coupon data** to observe their usage, identify patterns, and [evaluate the performance](/blog/sales-driven-dashboard) of your campaigns.
    
*   **Valuable customer insights:** VMS can provide data on customer behaviour and preferences, allowing for better targeting of future campaigns.

### **2\. Functional Requirements for the Coupon and Voucher Management System**

A robust VMS should offer a set of core functionalities:

#### Coupon/Discount Code Creation:

*   Allow administrators to create different discount code types with details like:
    
*   Name (e.g., "Welcome Offer")
    
*   Discount type (percentage, fixed amount, free service)
    
*   Discount value
    
*   The number of promotional codes available
    
*   Validity period (start and end dates)
    
*   Usage limits (one-time use, multiple uses)
    
*   Who can use it (all services, specific services, etc.)?
    

#### Campaign Management Functions:

*   Enable administrators to create targeted promotional code campaigns based on user demographics, booking history, or service preferences.
    

#### Distributing and Using Vouchers:

*   The system should allow for sending coupons through multiple digital channels, like email, SMS, or directly on the website. Additionally, redeeming coupons during the booking process should be straightforward and user-friendly.
    

#### Tracking and Reporting:

*   The system tracks the number of coupons used and identifies the most popular kind. It can also show how bookings change when offering coupons.
