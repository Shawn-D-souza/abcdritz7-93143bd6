### Quantifying and Calculating ROI

The bottom line is often the deciding factor when considering any new technology. So, how do you quantify the ROI of adopting a [low-code development platform](https://ritz7.com/blog/no-code-vs-traditional-dev-the-sme-advantage-in-2024)? Quantifying and calculating ROI involves assessing the returns generated from an investment relative to its initial cost.

By quantifying ROI, organizations can make informed decisions about resource allocation and prioritize investments that offer the highest potential for financial gain. The formula for calculating ROI is:  

**ROI = (Cost of Investment / Net Profit)×100%** 

*   **Where**:
    
*   Net Profit = Total Revenue - Total Costs
    

Total Investment = Initial Investment + Ongoing Costs
