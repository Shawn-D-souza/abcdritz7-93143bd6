That assumption is expensive.

n8n is a drag-and-drop tool. You connect apps by clicking blocks on a screen. No code. Developers use it, yes — but so do marketers, HR managers, freelancers, and small business owners. In 2026, learning n8n is closer to learning Excel than learning to code. It is a work skill. Anyone can pick it up.
