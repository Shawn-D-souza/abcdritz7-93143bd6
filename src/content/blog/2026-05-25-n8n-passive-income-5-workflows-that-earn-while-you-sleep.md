## What Is n8n Passive Income?

**n8n passive income** is money you earn from automated workflows you build on n8n – an open-source tool that connects apps and automates tasks. You can self-host it on your own server for as little as $5–$15/month, or use the cloud version starting at $26/month. Once you set up a workflow, it runs on its own — no extra work from you. It can earn money through selling templates, delivering data to clients, charging a monthly fee for a small software tool, or sending automatic alerts — all without you being involved every day.

> You build it once. It keeps earning for you.

*n8n is an open-source automation tool — free to self-host on your own server (you only pay for hosting, typically $5–$15/month), or available on the cloud starting at $26/month after a 14-day free trial, according to [n8n's official documentation](https://docs.n8n.io/).*

## 1. Sell Pre-Built n8n Workflow Templates

### Why This Is the Best Starting Point for Passive Income

A workflow template is like a ready-made recipe. You build it once, upload it to a marketplace, and anyone can buy and use it. After you upload it, every new sale costs you nothing — no calls, no extra work, no emails back and forth.



The demand is real. Thousands of business owners want to automate their work but do not know how to build it themselves. They are happy to pay for something ready to use. Automation and productivity templates consistently rank among the top-selling categories on [Gumroad](https://gumroad.com/), with successful sellers earning $500–$2,000/month from a single template pack.

The bottom line: one good workflow template, listed in the right place, can keep earning money for you indefinitely — with zero extra work after you upload it.

### What Workflows Sell Best

Focus on workflows that solve one clear, annoying problem that businesses deal with every week. Broad or vague templates do not sell. Specific ones do.

**Workflow templates that sell well:**

* **Social Media Schedulers**

   — Pull posts from Google Sheets and automatically share them on LinkedIn, Instagram, and X
* **E-Commerce Order Processors**

   — When a Shopify order comes in, update the CRM, tell the fulfilment team, and email the customer – all automatically
* **Lead Nurture Sequences**

   — When someone fills a form, send them personalised follow-up emails over 5 days without anyone lifting a finger
* **Invoice and Payment Reminders**

   — Connect to accounting software and send payment reminders automatically on due dates
* **CRM Lead Automation**

   — Grab new leads from anywhere, sort and score them, and push them straight into your sales pipeline
* **AI Review Responder**

   — Pull new Google Reviews, write a personalised reply using AI, and post it automatically

  **$27 – $97**
* Bundled kit (3–5 related workflows): 

  **$97 – $197**
* Industry-specific pack (e.g. "Real Estate Automation Kit"): 

  **$147 – $297**

### Where to Sell

   — easiest setup, instant delivery, handles payments globally

### How to Make It Sell

Your description matters more than the workflow itself. Talk about the result it delivers — not the technical parts inside it.

❌ *"This workflow uses a Sheets node connected to an OpenAI node and a LinkedIn node."*

✅ *"Turn one Google Sheet row into a week of social posts — automatically rewritten for LinkedIn, Instagram, and X. Set up in under 10 minutes. No coding needed."*

Think of each template as a small helper that works silently for whoever buys it – lead with that idea and your description will be much stronger.

One example from our own work: we built a workflow at Ritz7 that automatically generates YouTube video titles and descriptions using AI. That same workflow, packaged cleanly with a setup guide, could be sold as a template to any content creator or YouTube channel manager who does not want to write metadata by hand for every video.

## 2. Automated Lead List Delivery (Automation Arbitrage)

### The Idea: Sell the Output, Not the Workflow

Most n8n builders think about selling the workflow they built. This model is completely different — you sell what the workflow produces.

Here is how it works: you use n8n to automatically collect and organise useful information — like a list of potential customers, competitor prices, or market trends — and then deliver that to a paying client every week or every day. The client pays for the information. They never see or care about the tool behind it.

Tools like [Apollo](https://www.apollo.io/pricing) and ZoomInfo charge businesses $49–$149 per user every month just to access contact data — and that is before extra fees. You can offer the same kind of value at a much lower price and keep most of that as profit. One workflow like this can replace what a full lead generation agency does for a client — and it keeps earning you money every month without you touching it.

The client just gets a spreadsheet of 50 fresh contacts every Monday morning, automatically — like clockwork.





### Who Pays for This

Sales teams, recruiters, real estate agents, and marketing agencies all need a steady stream of new contacts to reach out to. Most of them are already paying a lot for this — through tools like Apollo or ZoomInfo, or by hiring agencies to find leads for them. You can offer the same results for less money. The same goes for market research — many companies pay monthly just to get a report on what competitors are doing, which your workflow can generate automatically.

### Example Workflows to Build

* **Local Business Lead Scraper**

   — Pull business listings from Google Maps for a specific city and type of business (e.g., "dental clinics in Austin"), add their contact details, and send as a simple file
* **LinkedIn Job Monitor**

   — Track new job postings in a specific field, collect company details, and send a weekly report to a recruiter
* **E-Commerce Store Tracker**

   — Watch for new online stores launching in a category and send contact details to a sales team
* **Competitor Price Monitor**

   — Check competitor product prices every day and email a short report to a retail client each morning

Once your lead list is ready, automate the outreach too — see [Automate Email Replies from Form Submission in n8n →](https://ritz7.ai/blog/automate-email-replies-form-submission-in-n8n)

### What to Charge

Price this as a subscription, not a one-time fee. Clients pay for the ongoing delivery, not just the data.

* **Weekly lead list (50 contacts):**

   $200 – $500/month
* **Daily monitoring report:**

   $300 – $800/month
* **Custom niche research:**

   $500 – $1,500/month

One client on a $300/month subscription is $3,600/year from a workflow you built in an afternoon.

### Important: Stay Compliant

Only collect information that is already publicly available online. Follow data privacy rules like GDPR. Always tell clients where the data comes from. This keeps your business honest and legal for the long run.
