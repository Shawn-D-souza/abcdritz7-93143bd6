## Main Features of Music Creator AI

  

### 1\. AI Music Generator

The AI Music Generator is the core engine behind the platform and one of its most advanced Text-to-Music systems. You can describe the music you want to create using a prompt, select the genre, mood, instruments, ambience, and more, and the AI generates full tracks in minutes. Whether you want lo-fi background music, cinematic ambience, upbeat rap beats, EDM, R&B, blues, reggae, jazz, or instrumental-only tracks, this tool adapts to any music style. You can choose to generate AI Songs with vocals or an instrumental-only version, depending on your needs. It produces multiple variations, ensuring you can pick the version that matches your project’s mood and purpose.

#### How to Use the AI Music Generator

1.  Click the **AI Music Generator** from the left menu.
    
    ![AI MUSIC](/assets/uploads/Dr6th6LLLMOKEyott1fgbuBZwqs.png)
    
      
    
2.  Choose **Instrumental Only** or **Own Lyrics** based on your project.
    
3.  Type a detailed prompt describing the atmosphere, music style, instruments, mood, and the desired sound duration.
    
4.  Select options under **#Genre**, **#Mood**, **#Instrument**, **#Ambience**, or **#Vocal Gender**.
    
5.  Click **Generate Music** to create your track. _text-to-music_
    
6.  Review the generated track under **Your Music History** and download or remix it.

    ![photo to music](/assets/uploads/n90rlw4fqxegMCyXXrSNxkzpok.png)
    
      
    
5.  Proceed to generate photo music to turn lyrics into a full track.

You can choose mood categories such as energetic, romantic, sad, happy, or reflective. Once the lyrics are ready, you can instantly use them in the AI Music Generator to turn them into a full song.

    ![ai lyrics](/assets/uploads/qnlCa4WAam9jyVtxdOIYypI0Ig.png)

3.  Choose your preferred **Mood**, **Style**, **Structure**, and **Language** to shape the type of lyrics the AI will generate.
    
4.  Click **“Generate Lyrics”** to produce a complete song, including verses, chorus, and other sections.
    
    ![ ai lyrics generator - use this lyrics t ogenerate ai music](/assets/uploads/7cNz3mgzaFNBYzID7apGAffE.png)

### 4\. Vocal Remover

If you need an instrumental version of a track, the **Vocal Remover** is extremely helpful. This tool separates vocals from the instrumental parts of any uploaded song with impressive accuracy. After uploading your audio file, the AI quickly provides both the isolated vocals and the instrumental-only version. This is perfect for karaoke, remixes, covers, or using clean audio in your videos. It functions similarly to advanced **AI vocal remover** tools and is also great for creating **sound effects**, backing tracks, or practice versions for musicians.

#### How to Use the Vocal Remover

1.  Click **AI Vocal Remover** from the sidebar.
    
    ![ai vocal remover](/assets/uploads/zjHb6RhZjyXSA0Nnk6c8MWWGu5s.png)
    
      
    
2.  Upload your audio file (MP3, WAV, etc.).
    
3.  Wait a few seconds for the AI to process the song.
    
4.  Switch between **Vocal** and **Instrumental** tabs to preview separated audio.
    
5.  Adjust volume sliders or mute/solo specific layers.
    
6.  Download the isolated vocals or instrumental-only tracks.
    

  

### 5\. AI Stem Splitter

In music production, “stems” are separate layers such as drums, bass, vocals, or instruments. The **AI Stem Splitter** analyzes a song and splits these layers into individual files for detailed editing. This is ideal for creators who want to split music or remix popular songs. It also helps those who study music composition by examining each musical component individually. You get access to key elements like drums, bass, vocals, and instruments with only one upload. This is especially useful for DJ mixing, music lessons, experimentation, and developing production skills.

#### How to Use the AI Stem Splitter

1.  Click the **"AI Stem Splitter"** on the left panel.
    
    ![ai step splitter - half](/assets/uploads/b1uqusH27y6nfwyaccGxUZocWw8.png)
    
      
    
2.  **Upload your audio track** (MP3, WAV, or another supported format).
    
3.  Click **“Separate Stems”** to let the AI split the song into individual components.
    
4.  After processing, **preview the separated stems** such as Bass, Drums, Vocals, and Other instrument layers.
    
5.  Use the interface to **mute, solo, or adjust the volume** of each stem as needed.
    
6.  **Download the individual stems** for remixing, production, DJ mixing, or learning purposes.
    

  

### 6\. AI Singing Voice Generator

This impressive feature allows you to create full songs using your own voice, even if you’re not a trained singer. The tool lets you upload samples of your voice so the AI can build a custom vocal model using vocal synthesis technology. Once the model is trained, you can generate songs, melodies, or even reinterpret existing tracks in your own voice. It’s an exciting way to explore different music styles, try creative ideas, or experiment with a virtual version of your voice without needing a recording studio.

#### How to Use the AI Singing Voice Generator

1.  Click the **AI Singing Voice Generator** from the left menu. _(See screenshot)_
    
    ![ai singing voice generator](/assets/uploads/bSGOHfZBfSllpppV5YFTmenaq8.png)
    
      
    
2.  Name your voice model and upload your audio samples (1–10 minutes recommended).
    
3.  Choose **Male** or **Female** for the voice gender.
    
4.  Select whether your model is **Public** or **Private**.
    
5.  Click **Create Voice** to begin training.
    
6.  Once ready, click **Create Music** to make songs using your voice model.
    

  

## 7\. AI Song Cover Generator

This feature builds on your trained voice model and lets you generate AI-powered covers of songs in your own voice. It’s a fun and interesting way to test how your voice would sound across different genres, tempos, and musical arrangements. Even skilled singers are often surprised by how accurately the AI captures tone, depth, and vocal personality. This makes it a valuable tool for creative exploration and expanding your musical style.

#### How to Use the AI Song Cover Generator

1.  Click the **"AI Song Cover Generator"** from the menu.
    
2.  From the available options, **select your trained AI voice model** that you want the cover to use.
    
3.  **Upload the original song** (MP3, WAV, or supported file) that you want the AI to recreate using your voice model.
    
4.  Choose the **singer's gender** setting — Male or Female — to match the intended vocal tone and performance style.
    
5.  Click **“Cover Your Music Now”** to begin generating the AI-powered cover version.
    
6.  Once the process is complete, **download the final AI cover** or remix/edit it further inside the platform.
    

  

### 8\. AI Music Mastering

Mastering is the important final step of music production, making tracks sound polished and professional. Music Creator AI automates this process beautifully. You can upload any track and use **Reference Audio** to match its tone, loudness, and clarity. This helps YouTubers, podcasters, and musicians get good audio quality. They do not need to use expensive gear. The tool balances your track, increases quality, and ensures the final sound is ready for sharing or publishing.

#### How to Use AI Music Mastering

1.  Click **"AI Music Mastering"** in the sidebar.
    
    ![ai music mastering half](/assets/uploads/QhBPPqGAdTXiesBZYVZfcadE8.png)
    
      
    
2.  **Upload your target audio file** — this is the track you want to enhance and professionally polish.
    
3.  _(Optional)_ **Upload a Reference Music file** if you want the AI to match the tonal balance, loudness, and style of another song.
    
4.  Wait a few moments and then **preview both the Mastered Audio and the Original Audio** to compare the difference in clarity, loudness, and depth.
    
5.  Once you’re satisfied, **download the mastered version** for use in videos, music projects, or publishing.
    

  

### 9\. MIDI Editor

Music Creator AI includes a powerful AI-backed **MIDI Editor** that lets you modify melodies and adjust musical ideas easily. It works alongside tools like the **AI MIDI Generator**, allowing both beginners and experienced musicians to experiment with notes, rhythms, chords, and overall structure. This hands-on approach helps users understand musical structures. It gives them flexibility to create or fine-tune melodies without needing advanced music theory knowledge.

#### How to Use the MIDI Editor

1.  Click the **AI MIDI Editor** from the menu.
    
    ![MIDI](/assets/uploads/QGxLBi4fYLfELYKQvVgytMt5Ll0.png)
    
      
    
2.  **Upload or open a MIDI file** (either your own or one generated by the AI Music Generator).
    
3.  Inside the piano-roll interface, **drag notes to change pitch**, move them horizontally to adjust timing, or delete notes you don’t want.
    
4.  **Add new notes, tracks, or instruments** to build harmonies, basslines, or melodies.
    
5.  Fine-tune your performance by adjusting **velocity, pitch bend, modulation, and expression** controls.
    
6.  When finished, **export the edited MIDI file** to use in your DAW or further refine in other Music Creator AI tools.
    

  

### 10\. AI Audio to MIDI

This tool transforms audio recordings into editable MIDI files. Whether it’s a humming idea, a short melody, or a part of a song, the AI captures its structure and turns it into MIDI notes you can modify. This feature is great for remixing, learning compositions, and turning raw musical ideas into clear, playable music. It’s especially helpful for working with different **music components**, practicing arrangement skills, or building new tracks from simple ideas.

#### How to Use AI Audio to MIDI

1.  **Click “AI Audio to MIDI”** from the left-side menu inside Music Creator AI.
    
2.  **Upload your audio source** (such as humming, singing, an instrument recording, or part of a song).
    
3.  The AI will process the file and **automatically extract the melody, pitch, and timing into MIDI notes**.
    
4.  Once the conversion is complete, **download the generated MIDI file**.
    
5.  (Optional) **Open the MIDI file in the built-in MIDI Editor** to edit notes, rearrange melodies, or adjust instruments.

When you upload any song, Music Creator AI instantly detects its key, alternate key, BPM (beats per minute), and energy level. This is very helpful for DJs syncing tracks for a smooth mix or for content creators who want music that perfectly matches transitions in **MP4 videos**, **MP3 audios**, or edited clips. The tool gives you precise information to help with timing and musical compatibility.

#### How to Use the Key & BPM Finder

1.  **Click “Key & BPM Finder”** from the left-side menu in Music Creator AI.
    
    ![KEY AND BPM FINDER](/assets/uploads/wsXWGXEOMall8pNHnKQE1dhjjA.png)
    
      
    
2.  **Upload your song file** (MP3, WAV, or supported audio format).
    
3.  Wait a few seconds while the AI analyzes the track.
    
4.  **View the detected values**, including:
    
    *   Key
        
    *   Alternate Key
        
    *   BPM (Beats Per Minute)
        
    *   Energy Level
        
    *   Danceability Score
        
    *   Happiness / Mood Indicators (depending on the tool version)
        
5.  **Use the extracted musical data** for mixing, beat-matching, transitions, or editing your project.
    

  

### 12\. BPM Tapper

This simple but useful feature lets you tap along to music with your keyboard or mouse, and the AI will calculate the exact tempo. This is helpful when structuring edits, planning beat drops, or identifying **sound duration** and rhythmic patterns. It’s a fast way to understand tempo without relying on advanced tools, making it ideal for beginners and creators who need a quick reference.

#### How to Use the BPM Tapper

1.  **Click “BPM Tapper”** from the left-side menu inside Music Creator AI.
    
    ![BPM TAPPER](/assets/uploads/494q0ufhX9xeBkCxDYCJytWdVws.png)
    
      
    
2.  **Start playing your music externally** (from Spotify, YouTube, your PC, etc.).
    
3.  **Tap your keyboard** (usually the **spacebar**) _or_ click the on-screen tap button in rhythm with the beat.
    
4.  The BPM will update live as you continue tapping.
    
5.  **Use the displayed BPM** for mixing, beat matching, editing timing, or syncing music in your project.
