## Step-by-Step Guide to Identifying Digital Artists

Suppose you want to know if an artist is real or AI; follow this roadmap. This will help you become a smarter music fan.

1.  **Check the Spotify Bio:** Read the Spotify bio carefully. Does it mention a real history, or is it vague and poetic like a Nostalgic Records release?  
    _The Why:_ Real humans have a history of playing in front of people that computers cannot fake easily.
    
2.  **Look at the music videos:** watch the music videos. If the artist's face never moves or looks like a painting, it might be **cloning sound** tech.  
    _The Why:_ Creating a 100% realistic moving human is still very hard for AI.
    
3.  **Search for a Digital Footprint:** Look for interviews on streaming services. If there are no videos of them talking, they are likely a digital creation.  
    _The Why:_ Real stars like **Kate Bush** or **Pet Shop Boys** have decades of interviews you can find.
    
4.  **Analyse the Album Covers:** Look closely at the album covers. AI often makes mistakes with fingers, eyes, or background patterns.  
    _The Why:_ AI art often has small "glitches" that a real photographer wouldn't leave in.
