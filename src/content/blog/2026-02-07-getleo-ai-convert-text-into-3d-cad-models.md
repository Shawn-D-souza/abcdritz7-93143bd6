## The Secret Weapon: The 9-Point Engineering Summary

This is where Get Leo AI leaves other AI tools in the dust. Most people forget that a product is more than just its shape. Leo AI generates a full project document that covers:

1.  **Introduction:** What are we building?
    
2.  **Product Overview:** High-level summary.
    
3.  **Mechanical Requirements:** Hinges, slots, and structural integrity.
    
4.  **Electrical Requirements:** (Correctly identified as purely mechanical—no fluff!).
    
5.  **Software Requirements:** (Correctly identified as not required).
    
6.  **Interfaces/Interactions:** Human ergonomics.
    
7.  **Environment & Conditions:** Durability and usage limits.
    
8.  **Safety & Compliance:** Regulatory and safety standards.
    
9.  **Appendices:** General drawing diagrams.
    

Download this as a PDF or Word doc. You can even export your notes to Google Docs for easier collaboration with your team. This level of technical documentation is what separates real product development from a weekend hobby.
